Frequently asked questions

Short answers about our process, timing, ownership, and costs.

How does the process work from start to finish?

We start with a quick chat to understand your needs. You can use our estimator for a ballpark price. Once we agree, we build and test your site, then deliver it live.

Open Estimator
How long does it take to get my website live?

Most projects go live within 2–4 business days. Larger or more complex sites may take 1–2 weeks.

What do I need to provide before you start?

Ideally your logo, text content, and any brand assets. The clearer your input, the faster the turnaround.

Can you help with text and images if I don’t have them ready?

We can suggest stock images and provide guidance, but it’s preferred if you bring your own images — your site will feel more authentic to your business.

Is the website mobile-friendly?

Yes, every site we deliver is responsive and looks good on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Will my site show up on Google?

Yes — we include SEO basics (clean code, fast load times, semantic markup, meta tags). For ongoing SEO campaigns, we recommend dedicated specialists.

What happens after the website is delivered?

You own your site fully. We hand over all files, and you can choose ongoing support or manage it yourself.

Can I edit the website myself later?

Yes. We include a CMS so you can edit text and images yourself. In the Business tier, we can also make changes and adjustments up to 1 week after delivery.

How much does it cost?

The estimator gives you a rough price range. The final price is confirmed after communicating with us.

Do you offer hosting, domain, and email setup?

Yes — but these services cost extra. We’ll choose the most affordable provider for your needs. You (the customer) pay for the hosting, domain, and email setup — not us.
